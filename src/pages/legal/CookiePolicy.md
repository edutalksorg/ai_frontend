# Cookie Policy for EduTalks

EduTalks uses cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience.

### 1. What are Cookies?
Cookies are small text files stored on your device that help us recognize you and remember your preferences.

### 2. How We Use Cookies
- **Authentication**: To keep you logged into your account.
- **Preferences**: To remember your language settings and theme choices.
- **Security**: To help identify and prevent security risks.
- **Analytics**: To understand how users interact with our platform (e.g., which lessons are most popular).

### 3. Managing Cookies
Most browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. However, disabling essential cookies may prevent some features of EduTalks from working correctly.

---
By using our site, you consent to our use of cookies as described above.
